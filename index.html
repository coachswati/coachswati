Product Strategy & Roadmap

Companies win or lose based on the quality of their products. I help you not just deliver products that customers love but also those that scale and drive your company strategy. We will work together on refining what “good” product strategy looks like, how should prioritization and recalibration be done and eventually how the product impacts short, mid and long term business outcomes!

Growth Hacking & Marketing

Businesses that aren’t growing, are dying. Growth Marketing teams are a combination of hackers and hustlers who believe in agile, evidence based and data driven marketing decisions. I help you build a marketing & growth strategy focussed on maximising business growth by making effective decisions across the entire funnel at the speed of data. We don’t abandon creative thinking but activate creative experiments and measure them to iterate and optimise.

Performance Coaching

I work with founders, leaders and their teams to unleash the incredible talent and potential of their employees. I coach founders on self awareness, emotional intelligence, building skills they need during the highs and the lows of their startup journey. Together, we do the deep work on crucial conversations, high stake decision making, building relationships and scaling their organisation and culture. I've worked with founders from pre-seed to Series A.

About Me

Hi! I’m Swati and I am a Product, Growth and Performance Coach. I have extensive experience across Product Management, Product Marketing, Growth, and leading high performing teams and orgs. I’ve worked alongside some brilliant leaders within large teams at Google and Meta but have also had a chance to build Products and teams from the ground up at Alibaba and 2 Startups in my career. Having worked at startups myself and mentored many founders in the last few years, I understand the context in which small businesses operate and what they need to grow and scale. I intuitively see the threads of opportunity that wind through a business, and endeavour to bring them together into a coherent whole, help teams extend their thinking, and drive material business advantage. I use this knowledge and practical experience while working with C-suite and leaders today across their Product, growth and most importantly, their people and performance strategy.
